Assignment 1: Due Wednesday, January 8

  • Complete and share a brief biography, using the template provided in the preceding link.
  • Submit via email a list of three or more pop culture artifacts (e.g., movies, television show episodes, music videos, manga, computer games, etc.), other than The Imitation Game, which discuss cryptography. Assignment 2: Due Friday, January 10

  • Math in Society—Cryptography (p. 403): Exercises #1–5, 7, 19, 20.
  • Additional problems:
    1. Use a Vigenère cipher with keyword BEAR to encrypt the word QUIET and decrypt the word GENTZ.
    2. Compute 56290 mod 73 by hand, explaining your method. (You may use your calculator to perform arithmetic.)
  • RSA worksheet: #1–3.
    Note: The phrase "mapping A to H" (for example) means the cipher which encrypts the plaintext letter A as the letter H.

You may send your response in the body of an email or as a Word attachment to an email, or share it with me as a Google Doc. If you send it as an attachment, please include your name in the document title.
